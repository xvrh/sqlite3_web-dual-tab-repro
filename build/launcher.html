sqlite3_web NoModificationAllowedError — manual repro

Click the button. It opens N sibling tabs of /index.html in one user gesture, all running their SqliteDatabase open concurrently. Their OPFS feature-detection probes race on _drift_feature_detection in OPFS root, surfacing as: 

NoModificationAllowedError: Failed to execute 'removeEntry' on 'FileSystemDirectoryHandle'

Open DevTools before clicking on each spawned tab — Chrome won't show the error otherwise. The error is logged by sqlite3_web itself as Error in worker: ….
